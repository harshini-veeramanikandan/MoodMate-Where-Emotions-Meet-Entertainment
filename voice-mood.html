How it works

Tap the mic, say how you feel — like "I feel really tired and just want to relax" — and MoodMate decodes your mood from your words.

Tips

Speak clearly and use emotion words. The more descriptive, the better the match.

Privacy

No audio is stored or sent anywhere. Transcription happens entirely in your browser via the Web Speech API.

Browser support

Works in Chrome and Edge. Firefox doesn't support the Web Speech API.
